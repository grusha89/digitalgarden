### **Quick Dates Entry**

Add logic to ROSSUM for entering dates similar to the workflow.

For example:
- When "28" is entered, it will autofill as the 28th day of the current month and year.
- When "080623" is entered, it will reformat to 8.6.2023.

Scenarios:
- 6 → 06.09.2023
- 06 → 06.09.2023
- 0609 → 06.09.2023
- 612 → 06.12.2023
- 123 → 12.03.2023
- 060923 → 06.09.2023
- 06092023 → 06.09.2023
